Meet the Band

Ryan Holmes

Lead Vocals
Cofounder of The Time Riders and former All-State vocalist, Ryan has a charismatic stage presence and powerful voice that captures the attention of audiences. A baritone masquerading as a tenor, the versatility of his vocals can change from gravelly to smooth from song to song and are the signature of the band.

David Wentzel

Lead Guitar and Vocals
Cofounder of The Time Riders and fingerstyle extraordinaire, Dave brings impeccable technique and over half a century of guitar experience to the ensemble. Being the cofounder and having both a son (Gavin) and a son-in-law (Ryan) in the group along with all his talent truly makes Dave the father of the band.

Gavin Wentzel

Keyboard and Vocals
Son of Dave and talented artist and songwriter of unique original music, Gavin adds depth and texture along with soulful vocal harmonies to the band. His mastery of the keys and energetic vocals are the featured acts throughout much of the group’s performances.

Jim Macmillan

Rhythm Guitar and Vocals
Proprietor of the band’s practice facility and rhythm wizard, Jim is one of three pillars of the solid foundation that keep the band together and the crowd moving. The infectious rhythms and incredible hospitality and positivity provided by Jim are imperative to the group’s success.

Chris Sideris

Bass Guitar
Provider of grooves and opponent to sheet music, Chris is another pillar of the foundation of the band’s powerful sound. Chris is truly the heartbeat of The Time Riders and his deep driving basslines bring essential rhythm and groove to the ensemble.

Alex Ames

Saxaphone, Percussion, Vocals
Saxophone ace and handheld percussion master, Alex is a talented vocalist and musician whose smooth vocal melodies and vibrant saxophone solos add a touch of class and excitement to the band’s performances. With Alex’s addition to the group, the band was able to add a wider range of songs to their repertoire.

Shirley Ladd

Guitar and Vocals
Experienced performer and captivating vocalist, Shirley adds invaluable experience and vast knowledge of the local music scene in the Finger Lakes. Having performed all over the area for many years, she brings unmatched class and unwavering musical aptitude to this upstart group. Her classic style and composed demeanor add another layer to the range of the band.

Bryan Bates

Drums
Rhythmic powerhouse and band logo designer, Bryan is the third pillar without which the band could not exist. Bryan found his love for the drums later in life but quickly developed a passion and skills for the kit. With his ability to keep the beat across diverse styles and genres, he is the true driver of the vehicle we call The Time Riders.
