Meet the Band

Ryan Holmes

Lead Vocals
Cofounder of The Time Riders and former All-State vocalist, Ryan commands attention with his dynamic vocal range. A baritone masquerading as a tenor, he effortlessly transitions from gravelly grit to smooth melodies, making his voice the signature sound of the band.

David Wentzel

Lead Guitar and Vocals
Cofounder and guitar virtuoso, Dave brings impeccable fingerstyle technique and over fifty years of musical experience to The Time Riders. With his son, Gavin, and son-in-law, Ryan, alongside him, Dave truly is the heart—and father figure—of the band.

Gavin Wentzel

Keyboard and Vocals
A gifted songwriter and versatile artist, Gavin enriches the band's sound with his masterful keyboard skills and soulful harmonies. His energetic performances and compelling original compositions add depth and texture, elevating each show.

Jim Macmillan

Rhythm Guitar and Vocals
Rhythm wizard and provider of the band’s rehearsal space, Jim lays down the infectious grooves that keep audiences moving. His boundless hospitality and positive energy are key ingredients in the group's chemistry and success.

Chris Sideris

Bass Guitar
Provider of grooves and opponent to sheet music, Chris is another pillar of the foundation of the band’s powerful sound. Chris is truly the heartbeat of The Time Riders and his deep driving basslines bring essential rhythm and groove to the ensemble.

Alex Ames

Saxaphone, Percussion, Vocals
A master of saxophone and handheld percussion, Alex brings smooth vocal melodies and vibrant solos that infuse sophistication and excitement into the band’s repertoire. His versatility has broadened the musical possibilities for The Time Riders.

Shirley Ladd

Guitar and Vocals
A seasoned performer with deep roots in the Finger Lakes music scene, Shirley provides captivating vocals and classic guitar stylings. Her extensive experience and composed stage presence add a layer of elegance and maturity to the band's diverse sound.

Bryan Bates

Drums
Rhythmic powerhouse and creator of the band's iconic logo, Bryan discovered drums later in life but quickly developed a passionate, driving style. His ability to keep impeccable rhythm across diverse musical genres makes him the engine driving The Time Riders.
